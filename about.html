About Us


PollSlaw is a simple, straightforward polling service website where people can gather to look, learn, and leave a little more informed about the world around them than when they arrived.

Everyone can view the polls, cast their vote, and view the results, but only registered users can create their own polls to gather insight, gain perspective, and just plain find out what other people are really thinking.

Want to validate your opinion? Verify a hunch? Or just ask a simple binary question to find out what those around you think about matters pertaining to society, science, entertainment, current events or any other issues.

But remember, calls for violence, using profanity, and threatening or disrespecting others is strictly prohibited and will result in the immediate removal and permanent banning of your account. So, ask away, but please do it in a manner consistent with an attitude of curiosity and inquiry.
